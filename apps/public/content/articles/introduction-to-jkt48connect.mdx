Welcome to JKT48Connect, the community-built API service designed to be the most reliable and complete source of JKT48 data for developers and fans alike. In this article, we'll explore why JKT48Connect is the ideal choice for anyone looking to build JKT48-powered applications while saving time, avoiding fragile scrapers, and getting access to real-time data from a single, consistent API.

## Why a Dedicated JKT48 API?

At JKT48Connect, we believe the JKT48 developer community deserves a proper foundation to build on. JKT48 data is scattered across multiple platforms — the official website, SHOWROOM, IDN Live, and YouTube — each with their own formats, rate limits, and undocumented internal APIs. Without a unified service, every developer building a bot, fanbase website, or mobile app has to solve the same data-collection problem from scratch. JKT48Connect solves this once, for everyone. You can explore our documentation and get started on [docs.jkt48connect.com](https://docs.jkt48connect.com).

## Why Choose JKT48Connect?

Our journey began with a simple frustration: building JKT48 fan applications required maintaining brittle scrapers that broke every time the official website updated its HTML structure. We set out to build a stable, versioned REST API that aggregates JKT48 data from all official sources and exposes it through a consistent, developer-friendly interface. As the project grew, we realized the opportunity to go far beyond basic member lists — and today JKT48Connect covers live stream data, theater schedules, official news, events, birthday tracking, video call schedules, and real-time chat streams, all in one place.

For a deeper look at how JKT48Connect compares to building your own data pipeline, see our article on [why developers should use JKT48Connect](/articles/why-developers-should-use-jkt48connect).

## What Can You Do with JKT48Connect?

JKT48Connect is a comprehensive data platform that covers every major category of JKT48 information:

-   **Member Data**: Access complete member profiles including photos, social media links, generation info, SHOWROOM room IDs, IDN usernames, and graduation status.
-   **Theater Schedules**: Retrieve upcoming and past theater show data including setlist names, member counts, seitansai celebrations, and show posters.
-   **Live Streams**: Monitor real-time live stream activity across IDN Live, SHOWROOM, and YouTube simultaneously from a single endpoint.
-   **Recent Live**: Access past stream data with viewer counts, gift statistics, and stream duration for analytics and leaderboard features.
-   **News & Announcements**: Fetch official JKT48 news and announcements, categorized and paginated, as soon as they are published.
-   **Events**: Track upcoming JKT48 off-air shows, concerts, and fan events with date and detail links.
-   **Birthday Tracking**: Get upcoming member birthdays to power celebration notifications and seitansai features in your app.
-   **Video Call Schedules**: Display VC event schedules for fans planning their sessions.
-   **Chat Streams**: Tap into real-time IDN Live and SHOWROOM chat data for live interaction features.
-   **npm Package**: Use `@jkt48/core` for a fully typed, method-based client in Node.js and TypeScript projects.

## Commitment to the Community

Community and reliability are at the core of JKT48Connect's philosophy. We believe that the JKT48 developer ecosystem is stronger when everyone builds on a shared, well-maintained foundation rather than reinventing the wheel with individual scrapers. JKT48Connect is built by fans for fans — maintained by the JKT48ConnectCORP team with input from the developers and fanbase groups who use it every day. We are trusted by Discord bots serving thousands of members, official fanbase websites, and fan-built mobile applications across the Indonesian JKT48 community.

## Your Access, Your Control

At JKT48Connect, your project's needs come first. Standard API keys are available for individual developers and fan projects, with rate limits suitable for bots and personal apps. For verified organizations — official fanbase groups, certified developers, and accredited media partners — Priority Access is available free of charge, removing rate limits entirely so high-traffic applications can serve their communities without interruption. You can delete your key or switch tiers at any time, with no vendor lock-in.
